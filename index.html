Beginners:

This class is for you if you have little or no experience of swimming.

Basic:

This class is for you if you have completed Beginners and can swim but are not confident in deep water and working towards building your confidence.

Intermediate:

This class is for you if you have completed Beginners and are confident in deeper water and progressing towards moving into the Advanced classes.

Advanced:

This class is for you, if you can swim a full length of the pool confidently and want to improve your swim technique.
